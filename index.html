US Economy (1975–2024)

Inflation (YoY), unemployment rate, and GDP (with YoY growth) — minimal data visualization with Vega-Lite.

Overview

The dataset is quarterly. Unemployment is the quarterly average (from monthly UNRATE), inflation is YoY change of quarterly-average CPI, and GDP is quarterly level with YoY growth. This project is exploratory and does not claim causality.
